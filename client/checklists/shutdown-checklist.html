📝 Shutdown Checklist

More info: KROO SOP

1 🌀 Switch Off A/C
Check the electricity credit balance and note the amount.
2 🎛 Check All Remote Controls
Check that all remote controls are there
3 🪑 Check Chairs Count
Check the count of chairs in every room.
4 🔎 Check Forgotten Items
Check for forgotten items.
5 📹 DVR / Cameras
Check if the DVR and cameras are operational and reset if needed.
6 🔋 Disconnect Battery
Disconnect battery from charging
7 💸 Count Cash
Count cash in the front desk
8 📱 Put KROO Phone in charger
Put KROO phone in charging.
9 💡 Switch Off Lights
Switch off all lights.
